About me...

Hello, my name is Lily Foster-Grellis. I'm passionate about creating visually appealing and responsive websites, and my focus is on designing and building websites that are not only aesthetically pleasing but also user-friendly and accessible.


During my bootcamp, I learned and worked on a variety of front-end development technologies and tools, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and Bootstrap. I also gained experience with various design tools like Figma, Sketch, and Adobe XD.


One of my favorite aspects of front-end development is the ability to see my work come to life in a tangible way. I enjoy creating interactive and engaging user interfaces and am always looking for ways to improve my skills and techniques.


My approach to front-end development is collaborative and detail-oriented. I believe in working closely with clients and stakeholders to ensure that the end product meets their needs and exceeds their expectations. I am also committed to staying up-to-date with the latest trends and advancements in front-end development to provide the best possible user experience.
